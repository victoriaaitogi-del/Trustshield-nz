# TrustShield NZ 🛡️

New Zealand's first verified business trust platform and AI prompt injection protection system.

## What It Does

**1. Prompt Injection Scanner**
Detects manipulative copy-paste prompts being circulated online to trick AI systems into producing harmful content. Built after a real prompt injection attack was identified being shared in NZ communities.

**2. Verified Business Directory**
A Gold/Silver/Bronze business rating system where every reviewer is identity-verified. Unlike Trustpilot, TrustShield uses a dialogue-first resolution process — businesses can respond to reviews, disputes go to mediation, and bad-faith reviews are removed.

**3. Size-Fair Scoring**
A micro business with 8 perfect reviews scores fairly against a national chain with 400 reviews. Small businesses are not penalised for having fewer customers.

## Built For New Zealand
Developed in response to real AI manipulation attacks circulating in NZ. Seeking partnership with InternetNZ, Consumer NZ, and NZ CERT.
